Know what you can carry on

There are some items that can only travel in your carry-on bag (and some items that must be checked).Make sure you know where to pack these resticted items-and if you can carry them on.

Forbidden items

Defensive spray:

  • Mace
  • Tear-gaz
  • pepper spray

Explosives:

  • fireworks
  • gunpowder
  • flares/flare guns
  • swingless golf club load strips

Firearms or ammunition:

  • handguns
  • rifles
  • shotguns
  • ammunition
PS: you can travel with firearms in checked in bags only if they are declared to an agent at check-in.

Flammable liquids or solids:

  • fuel
  • paints
  • gas torches (including micro-torches and torch lighters)
  • flammable glues/epoxies

Household items:

  • bleach
  • spray strap
  • insecticides
  • drain cleaners
  • solvents
  • aerosols
  • oven or bathroom cleaners
  • ...

Hoverboards:

  • hoverboards
  • balance wheels
  • electric small scooters
  • intelligent scooters

Drugs

Alcoholic beverages over 70 percent by volume

Self-heating meals

Items allowed in carry-on bags

Batteries:

  • 9-volt
  • AA/AAA/C/D
  • Alkaline
  • carbon zink
  • silver oxide
  • lithium or lithium-ion
  • zink air
  • nickel-cadmium or nickel-metal hydride
PS: Please remove batteries from devices in your checked bags and put them in your carry-on in separated plastic bags.

Gasoline-powered tools:

  • gas-powered trimmers/edgers
  • chain saws

Drones:

  • battery must not exceed 160wh
  • carried on if its box is less than 22x14x9 inches / 56x36x23 cm

Electronic cigarettes:

  • Vapor cigarettes
  • e-cigarettes
PS: You can travel with them in your check bags, you can travel with them in your carry-on , but you're not allowed to use them onboard any flight.

Lighters and matches:

  • matches
  • lighters or lighter fluid
  • Grill lighters

Personal care items:

  • Aerosol deodorant or hair spray
  • insect repellent
  • perfume or cologne containing alcohol
  • nail polosh or nail remover
  • large bottles of acetone such as liquid nails

Items allowed in checked bags

Alcoholic beverages under 70 percent by volume

  • Beer
  • wine
  • other alcoholic beverages in retail packaging
PS: you can take a max of 5L per passanger for a beverages 24-70 percent alcohol by volume in checked bags

Cutting instruments :

  • metal Knives(of any length or type)
  • box cultters
  • ice picks
  • straight razors

Stun guns/Shocking devices

  • taser devices
  • Elctro-shock weapons (conducted electrical weapons)
  • Stun guns
PS:You can travel with t5hem in your carry-on bags but you must remove all batteries from devices & put them in seperated plastic bags.
